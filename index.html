    Dr. Kiran Khurshid

    Acting as Director General(DGM&E)

    I am Dr. Kiran Khurshid (PAS), a dedicated academic and researcher in Political Science. I earned my Ph.D. from Government College University, Lahore, in 2018, focusing on an evaluation of land revenue. My M.Phil. research explored Dependencia: A Case Study of Pakistan (1981-1991), and I hold a Master of Arts in Political Science from the same institution. My academic journey has been centered on governance, international relations, and political thought, shaping my expertise in political analysis and research.

    March 2025 : Sadequain Gallery - An Ode to the Artist

    https://www.facebook.com/share/18vXyNsxLn/?mibextid=wwXIfr

    I am absolutely delighted to share the momentous occasion of the inauguration of my book, Sadequain Gallery – An Ode to the Artist, in a prestigious ceremony held at the esteemed National School of Public Policy on 20th March 2025. People from the art, academia and civil service attended the event. It is both a great privilege and an immense honour to have been entrusted with this endeavour by the Honourable Rector, Dr Ijaz Munir. Both the establishment of Sadequain Gallery by him in January 2023 and now documentation of this book on 20th March 2025 are testament to his dedication to the art and culture.
